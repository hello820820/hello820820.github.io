05Projects
Minimum Stable Grip Force Control via Tactile-Guided Future Slip Prediction 2026.03 — 06
Team project · “Intelligent Robotics” course
A two-stage pipeline for grasping deformable objects: Stage 1 predicts future slip probability (next 15 steps) by fusing tactile (ViT-Small), vision (ResNet-18), and proprioception via modality-dropout fusion and a 2-layer LSTM; Stage 2 uses it as a real-time signal for closed-loop grip-force control — deciding when to act under uncertain contact. Built in Isaac Sim with TacEX (GelSight Mini). Multimodal fusion proved most robust under camera occlusion and generalized best to unseen objects. demo video ↗
